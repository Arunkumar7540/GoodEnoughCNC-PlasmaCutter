WHAT?

CNC plasma for micro/small production line and prototiping.

WHY?

To serve as support steel cutting machine in CNC, metalworking shops, prototiping companies and home use.

HOW?

Construct your own units from a kit, soon built units will be available through global micro-manufacturing network.

Build Your Own

A DIY friendly open-source release together with a complete kit of assembly parts as well as detailed instructions on how to properly assemble and test the system is avaliable. Design in suitable for almost everyone since almost no prior experience in building is needed. Build your one GoodenoughCNC Plasma in just a few days with only basic tools found in any garage. For more information and enquiries please contact us.
