WHAT?

CNC machines for micro/small production line and prototyping.

WHY?

To serve as support machines in CNC, metalworking shops, prototyping companies and home use.

HOW?

Construct your own units from a kit, soon built units will be available through global micro-manufacturing network.

Build Your Own

A DIY friendly open-source release as well as detailed instructions on how to properly assemble and test the system is available. Design is suitable for almost everyone since almost no prior experience in building is needed. Build your own GoodEnoughCNC machine in just a few days with only basic tools found in any garage. For more information and enquiries please contact us.

Get Involved

Since whole project is open-source, we encourage everybody to help us improve our GoodenoughCNC machines and make them even better and cheaper.
