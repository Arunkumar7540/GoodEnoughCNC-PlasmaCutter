Features

Very low cost design

GoodEnoughCNC plasma is built from standard mechanical componets and steel profiles, which makes good enough CNC plasma afordable. Just basic handtools needed for assembly.

Frame

Suitable for almost any application refering to the size of material. Because of the modular built the cutting dimensions can be increased just with replacing only main structural beams.

Z axis

Lighter and simpler to manufacture, the Z axis mechanism does not require milled parts like conventional solutions. We're using standard beraing slide guides avaliable in local hardware store.

Open-source and open-hardware

Published under open-source open-hardware licenses. You can share or modify it for any purpose, even commercially.

Plasma

The Chinese BMF CUT 50M plasma cutter packs more than enough punch to cleanly cut up to 5 mm sheet metal (10 mm max cut). Additionally a compressor and torch height controller is needed. You can replace with plasma of your choice.

DESIGN

The main guideline for building a CNC Plasma was its easy construction and material, avaliable from the local hardware store with use of only basic hand tools. The entire frame is made from a rectangular 40x40 mm pipes which simultaneously serve as guides for the X and Y axis. For trolleys we used 100x100mm rectangular pipes which are embedded by standard 608ZZ bearings. The drive was carried out with a timing belt, which is driven by Nema stepper 23 No. It has been shown that for the chosen design we are able to achieve good accuracy and aligment of parts during construction of the system and at the same time ensures a very good accuracy and repeatability during its operation. Z axis is built in a way that offers maximum rigidy to a plasma gun and minimalizes vibration during operation.

SYSTEM ASSEMBLY

We constructed CNC plasma which can be assembled by people with very little building experience. There is no need for complex and expensive tools. Majority of construction can be build from steel plates and beams with basic operation like drilling and cutting. All plastic parts are 3D printed. We prepared extensive instructions for assembly and guides for properly drilling holes and cutting steel profiles.
